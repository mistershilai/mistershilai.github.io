Welcome! This is the first post on the rebuilt site. The old site was plain
HTML + Bootstrap; this version runs on **Astro** with **Vercel** serverless
functions for the contact form.

## What's new

- **Dark mode** with a persisted preference
- **Command palette** (press `⌘K` anywhere) for quick navigation
- **3D interactive globe** on the homepage with places I've visited
- **Page transitions** — watch the URL change without the flash
- **Filterable project cards** with hover effects
- **MDX blog** so I can mix prose, code, and plots

## What's next

I'll use this space to write about research — supply-chain optimization work
in Botswana, the population-genetics modeling project, and occasional notes
from EMT shifts and travel.

Thanks for visiting. Email me via the [contact page](/contact) or press `⌘K`.
