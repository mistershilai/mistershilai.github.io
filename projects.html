Research & Projects

Global Health Systems
Antimicrobial Supply Chain Optimization (ACHAP)
Leading the development of supply chain models for Botswana's Ministry of Health, integrating distance matrices with economic and epidemiological data. Presented proposal to government officials at the Central Medical Stores and Ministry of Health to improve antimicrobial distribution networks across the country. Under supervision of Dr. Bartolomeo Stellato (ORFE), with collaborators from ACHAP (Mr. Lesego Busang, Mr. Stanley Mapiki, Mr. Tiro Molefe, Dr. Khumo Seipone)
Vaccination Program Cost-Effectiveness Analysis
RA to Dr. Giridara Gopal and Dr. Ramanan Laxminarayan with the One Health Trust in Karnataka to model vaccination program cost-effectiveness through Markov chain simulations. Contributor to policy briefs for Pakistan (primary writer), South Africa, Nepal, Uganda, and Nigeria. Contributor to presentations for Tanzania, Zimbabwe, India, Kenya, South Africa, Vietnam, Mozambique, Laos, and Nigeria.
Healthcare Technology
Organ Donation Allocation Tool (In Development)
Developing a machine learning-based tool to optimize organ donation matching and allocation. The system aims to enable physicians to immediately identify compatible organ matches in their geographic area, reducing time-to-transplant and improving patient outcomes through real-time compatibility analysis.
Biostatistics & Genomics
Population Genetics Research
Currently conducting research at Princeton's Lewis-Sigler Center for Integrative Genomics under Dr. Olivia Harringmeyer and Dr. John Storey. Using SLiM to model fixed genetic mutation effects in populations under migration conditions. Developing a research paper on chromosomal inversions under three-loci continent-island extension to established models.
Economic Research
RA to Samya Aboutajdine
Analyzed 10 mil+ building permits alongside FEMA risk data to examine housing disaster resilience. Conducted regression analyses of local resilience investments on FEMA grant flows to understand intervention effectiveness.
RA to Dr. Janet Currie
Contributed to research on adolescent social programs through data cleaning and visualization. Work acknowledged in Working Paper 33632.
RA to Dr. Sylvain Chassang
Reweighted survey data to correct for sampling bias in French transportation studies.
